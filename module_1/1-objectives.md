# Module 1: SaaS Fundamentals & Business Models

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will:
1. Understand what differentiates SaaS from traditional software
2. Know the core architectural principles that define SaaS applications
3. Understand various SaaS business models and their technical implications
4. Be able to identify key SaaS metrics and why they matter for technical decisions
5. Understand multi-tenancy at a conceptual level
6. Know how to evaluate technology choices for SaaS applications
